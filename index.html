Objective

My mission is to take part in the advancement of technology and the acceleration of innovation. Moore's law states that computing power doubles every 2 years, but this does not occur on its own. Software and hardware engineers alike must foster their knowledge to collaborate and create a compelling product. Each new release is better than the last, leaving humanity with overwhelming power which was never before possessed. Being able to communicate with someone across the world in an instant, or having refrigerators to encapsulate and preserve our food would leave our ancestors in admiration. It is through ones and zeros that software will be indistinguishable from magic.
